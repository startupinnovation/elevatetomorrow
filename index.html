Turn Your Old Tech into Tools for Learning

Why Your Donation Matters

Every year, millions of electronic devices end up in landfills, harming our planet and wasting valuable resources. Meanwhile, millions of students worldwide lack access to essential learning tools.

At Elevate Tomorrow, we collect your used electronics, refurbish or recycle them responsibly, and use the proceeds to support education, including:

  • Providing laptops and tablets to students in underserved communities.
  • Supplying essential school resources to underfunded classrooms.
  • Supporting STEM education programs for low-income youth.

What We Accept

  • 📱 Smartphones
  • 📲 Tablets
  • 💻 Laptops
  • 🖥️ Desktop Computers
  • 🔌 Chargers & Accessories

We’ll guide you through wiping your data safely before donating.
